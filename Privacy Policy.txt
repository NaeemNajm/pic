Al-Hikmah Tour & Travels - Privacy Policy
Last Updated: October 7, 2025

Al-Hikmah Tour & Travels is committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share the information of our respected pilgrims and clients. By using our services, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

1. What Information We Collect

To provide you with your desired services, such as Umrah or Hajj package bookings, visa processing, and other travel-related services, we may collect the following information:


Personal Identification Information: Your name, address, mobile number, and email address.




Travel and Booking Information: Passport number, issue and expiration dates, date of birth, nationality, and biometric information (such as fingerprints) which are essential for visa processing.


Payment Information: Your payment method (e.g., bank transfer, mobile banking, or cash), transaction history, and in some cases, bank account details.



Communication Information: When you communicate with us via phone, email, or other channels, we may keep a record of that conversation or message.

2. How Your Information is Used

We use your information for the following purposes:


Service Provision: To confirm your package booking, submit your Umrah visa application, and complete air ticket and hotel bookings.





Communication: To inform you about your booking status, payment confirmations, travel itineraries, and other important updates.


Governmental Obligations: To provide necessary information as required by the regulations of the governments of Bangladesh and Saudi Arabia.

Customer Service: To answer any of your questions or resolve issues and to provide overall support.

3. Information Sharing and Third Parties

To successfully organize your journey, we are required to share some of your information with trusted third parties. This includes:


Airlines: To issue your flight tickets.




Hotels: To confirm your accommodation in Makkah and Madinah.





Relevant Authorities in Saudi Arabia: For the issuance of your Umrah visa and for entry permits into the country.




Local Transport Companies: To arrange vehicle services for airport transfers and Ziyarah.


We assure you that we only share necessary information with these third parties, and they are permitted to use this information solely for the purpose of delivering the contracted services.

4. Data Security

The security of your personal information is our highest priority. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of your data.

5. Data Retention Period

We retain your information for a specific period as required by legal obligations and business necessities.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access: The right to receive a copy of the information we hold about you.

Correction: The right to request the correction of any inaccurate information.

Withdrawal: You may withdraw your consent for us to use your information at any time (however, this may prevent us from providing certain services).

7. Changes to the Policy

We may change or update this Privacy Policy from time to time due to business or legal requirements. Any changes will be published on our website.
